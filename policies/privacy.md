# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Flying Skate is an arcade skateboarding game for iOS.

## Information We Collect

Flying Skate does not require an account and does not directly collect personal information from players.

The app stores a small amount of gameplay data locally on the device, such as:

- Local best score
- Audio mute preference
- Gameplay settings and run state needed for normal play

This local data stays on the device unless the player chooses to share something using iOS features.

## Game Center

Flying Skate supports Apple Game Center leaderboards.

If the player is signed in to Game Center, the app may submit the player's final run score to the Game Center leaderboard. Game Center is provided by Apple and is governed by Apple's terms and privacy practices.

Playing Flying Skate does not require signing in to Game Center.

## Sharing

Flying Skate includes a share-score feature. When the player chooses to share a score card, the iOS share sheet lets the player choose the destination app or service.

Flying Skate does not receive the player's account information from those third-party services.

## Advertising And Tracking

Flying Skate does not include third-party advertising and does not track players across apps or websites.

## Purchases

Flying Skate is a paid upfront app and does not currently include in-app purchases or subscriptions.

## Children

Flying Skate is designed as a general audience game. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
