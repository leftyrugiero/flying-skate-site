## About Flying Skate

Flying Skate is a paid upfront iOS arcade skate runner. The game is designed for quick runs, clean jumps, grinds, tricks, and leaderboard chasing.

## Common Questions

### Do I need Game Center to play?

No. You can play Flying Skate without signing in to Game Center.

Game Center is only used for leaderboards. If you are signed in, Flying Skate can submit your final run score after game over and show the Rankings screen.

### Why are Rankings unavailable?

Rankings require Apple Game Center. Check that you are signed in to Game Center in iOS Settings and that your device has network access.

### I cannot hear music or sound effects.

Check the in-game mute button, your device volume, Silent Mode, Focus settings, and whether another audio route such as Bluetooth headphones is active.

### Does the app include in-app purchases?

No. Flying Skate is planned as a paid upfront app and does not currently include in-app purchases or subscriptions.

### How do I report a bug?

Email support.flyingskate@gmail.com with:

- Device model
- iOS version
- App version
- What happened
- Steps to reproduce the issue, if possible
